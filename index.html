About Me

My name is Matthew Garcia. I was born and raised in sunny SoCal. I started my journey in photography at the age of 20. I take photos so that I can share the amazing places I travel to, the beautiful people I meet, and the unforgettable moments in life. A camera can capture a photo of exactly what you see through the lense in a particular moment in time. Memories are past experiences and data stored in the brain. The thing I find most beautiful about photography is that when you create a photo, it's like you are bringing memories to life. My goal as a photographer is to create and capture as many memories as I can.
